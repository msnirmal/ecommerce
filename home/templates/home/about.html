ABOUT US

Bookstore story begins with six friends who worked in the book trade for more than 10 years and all share a passion for reading. They believed the kids these days are mostly engaged in social media/ online games / videos and the enjoyment of reading books have diminished. So they decided to recapture its spirit with an ambitious venture to establish an alternative online bookshop, especially for kids.

Started in 2020 we are one the leading online book store

Our mission is to provide you with best books for your kids to nurture their creativity
